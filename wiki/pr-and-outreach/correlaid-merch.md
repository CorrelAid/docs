# CorrelAid Merch

There are several stickers and postcards from CorrelAid. If you want to get some, e.g. for a local chapter event, then feel free to reach out to [info@correlaid.org](mailto:info@correlaid.org).

We have a cooperation with [dezent.me](https://dezent.me), a small German company who embroider t-shirts with our logo. You can purchase a shirt from the [dezent.me shop](https://dezent.me/c/projects/correlaid).
