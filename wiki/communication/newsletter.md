# Newsletter

## For Volunteers

By subscribing to our **volunteer newsletter** you will not only receive the monthly CorrelNews – packed with info and updates from our Data4Good community – but also the calls for applications for our Data4Good projects and invitations to great CorrelAid events. You can also subscribe to updates from CorrelAidX local groups in your area. You can also find current data science job postings here and share job postings for your company (please write to [info@correlaid.org](mailto:info@correlaid.org)). You can [subscribe here](https://dashboard.mailerlite.com/forms/257414/78748110521631819/share).

## For Non-Profits

In our monthly German **newsletter for non-profit organizations**, we give insights from our work: past Data4Good projects, upcoming events and educational workshops, stories from our community and the newest PR activities. You can [subscribe here](https://dashboard.mailerlite.com/forms/257414/78742752988235359/share).
